About St. Louis Jewelers

Based in St. Louis, Missouri

St. Louis Jewelers (Joyería) specializes in jewelry repair, restoration, and custom made pieces. From your idea to CAD design, casting, and polishing, we bring your vision to life.

Founded by Arturo Tobias Sr. in 1992, the business began as a small repair shop in the Chicagoland area before expanding and relocating to St. Louis, Missouri with the help of his son, Arturo Jr. Together, they helped grow the shop into what it is today. Arturo Sr. learned the craft of jewelry-making in Mexico from his family of jewelers.

Today, that family tradition and experience are reflected in every piece we create.
