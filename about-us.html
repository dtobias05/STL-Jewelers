About St. Louis Jewelers

Based in St. Louis, Missouri

St. Louis Jewelers (Joyería) specializes in expert restoration and repair services, as well as crafting one-of-a-kind pieces. From concept to CAD design, through casting and polishing, we bring your vision to life. Founded by Arturo Tobias Sr. in 1992, the shop originally started as a small repair business in the Chicagoland area before relocating and expanding in St. Louis, Missouri. Arturo Sr. began his jewelry-making journey in Mexico at a young age, learning the craft from his family of skilled jewelers. This rich heritage and expertise are reflected in the exceptional craftsmanship seen in every piece we create.
