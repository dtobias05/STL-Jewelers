About St. Louis Jewelers

Welcome to St. Louis Jewelers (Joyería), where every piece tells a story of artistry and passion. We take pride in our restoration and repair work, transforming your visions into unique heirlooms through stunning CAD designs, meticulous casting, and expert polishing. Founded by Mr. Tobias Sr. in 1992, our journey began in the heart of Chicago as a small repair shop, shortly after Arturo made the bold decision to immigrate to the U.S. in 1979.

With deep roots in Mexico, Arturo started his jewelry-making journey as a child, learning the delicate craft from his family of skilled artisans. This rich heritage shines through in the exceptional quality and craftsmanship of each piece we create.

Now nestled in the vibrant St. Louis community, we're dedicated to building lasting relationships with our clients. Your satisfaction isn’t just a goal; it's our mission. We work closely with you to ensure that every creation is a true reflection of your unique style and personality. Experience the passion and artistry at St. Louis Jewelers—where your dreams become reality, one exquisite piece at a time.
